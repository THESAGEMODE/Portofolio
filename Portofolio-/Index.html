United Barber Cashier

United Barber Cashier is a Point of Sale (POS) system specifically designed to help barbershop operations become faster, more organized, and more efficient. This system allows cashiers to select various available services and products, such as haircuts, shaving, hair washes, hair color, and hair care packages, and then add them to the customer's order list. With a modern and easy-to-use interface, cashiers can search for services, manage order quantities, apply discounts, calculate taxes automatically, and view total payments in real time. Furthermore, this system is equipped with management features for products, service categories, transaction reports, customer data, employee attendance, and system settings. United Barber Cashier helps improve service speed, reduce transaction recording errors, and simplify the digital and professional management of your barbershop business.
